Arcade Game

Using your arrow keys to reach to the other side in 60 seconds! If you reach destination, you gain 1 point!

Collect following items

  • Blue Gem - Bugs' speed decrease 1 second!
  • Green Gem - 2 points!
  • Orange Gem - 5 points!
  • Heart - one life!

Avoid following items

  • Bug - lose one life!
  • Water - lose one life!
  • Rock - lose one life!
